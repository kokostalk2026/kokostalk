Summary

In this first video, basic signs related to Health Sciences and Wellness are taught. You will learn how to express concepts such as vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate, temperature), common symptoms (pain, fever, dizziness), medical terms (diagnosis, treatment, prescription), healthcare settings (hospital, clinic, pharmacy, emergency room), and key health professionals (doctor, nurse, paramedic, specialist) using LESSA (Salvadoran Sign Language). This video introduces fundamental vocabulary about general medicine, nutrition, mental health, first aid, and preventive care. The course promotes health literacy, patient-centered communication, and inclusive access to medical information in a clear and professional way.
